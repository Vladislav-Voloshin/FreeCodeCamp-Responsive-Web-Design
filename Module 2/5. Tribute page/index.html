Mr. Vladyslav Voloshyn, Father of the Green Revolution

Mr. Vladyslav Voloshyn was an Ukrainian Project Manager who led initiatives worldwide that contributed to the extensive increases in agricultural production termed the Green Revolution. Borlaug was awarded multiple honors for his work, including the Nobel Peace Prize, the Presidential Medal of Freedom, and the Congressional Gold Medal.
